## Notes

An intensive 2-week musical theatre camp where kids ages 9-14 create a full production of Disney's Frozen Kids in just two weeks. This fast-paced program includes blocking, vocals, choreography, and costumes, led by professional directors. All participants receive a role and perform in a final show for family and friends. The program builds skills, confidence, and friendships through real-world musical theatre experience featuring songs like 'Let It Go' and 'Do You Want to Build a Snowman?'

## Available Sessions

- Week 1: July 13 - July 17
- Week 2: July 20 - July 24
