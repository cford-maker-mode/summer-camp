## Notes

Camp Orkila is a historic YMCA camp established in 1906, located on 280+ acres of beautiful beachfront property in the San Juan Islands. Offers both overnight camps for grades 3-12 and themed day camps for grades 1-6, plus weekend family camps. Activities include archery, boating, arts & crafts, zipline, climbing wall, challenge course, and environmental education at their marine center. Features cabin lodging, meals with dietary options, and operates on YMCA values of caring, honesty, respect, and responsibility. Uses a sliding fee scale to ensure accessibility for all families.
